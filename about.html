At Readower, we believe in the transformative power of reading. Our journey began with a passion for literature and a commitment to make reading accessible to everyone, regardless of their challenges.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a welcoming space where readers of all ages and abilities can discover the joy of books. We are dedicated to providing a diverse and inclusive collection of titles that cater to different interests and reading needs, including dyslexic readers. We also aspire to include educational books specialized in different areas of university studies in the future. We hope that Readower will become part of the Minister of Education's program to help dyslexic kids excel in their studies.

What Sets Us Apart

Dyslexia-Friendly Focus

We are proud to offer a specialized selection of dyslexia-friendly books, carefully chosen for their readability and accessibility.

Community and Support

Beyond books, we foster a supportive community where readers can share their experiences, find resources, and connect with others who share their passion for reading.

Educational Resources

We provide tools and resources to empower readers, educators, and parents in supporting dyslexic readers and enhancing reading skills.

We are committed to continuous improvement and innovation, always striving to expand our offerings and improve our services based on feedback and research.

Join us in our mission to promote literacy and inclusivity. Whether you're a reader, educator, parent, or advocate, there's a place for you in our community. Thank you for being a part of our story.
